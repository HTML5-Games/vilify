Unleash Your Inner Mad Scientist and Destroy the World (with Ice Cream) in Vilify, an Open Source HTML5 Game!
In Vilify, you are a Mad Scientist who has just completed a doomsday device. Now all your diabolical schemes from your wildest dreams are within reach! Nobody can stop you, and the the UN is powerless against and has no choice but to give into your demands... they must give you 10000 pounds of ice cream, or you will destory the world. Vilify is currently a work in progress.

Help build Vilify!
Vilify is an open source project, which means that everyone can help build it. Whether you're a programer, artist, or someone who has an idea for something they think would be really cool in this game, we want (and need) your help. In fact we order you, in the name of ice cream, to help us! Checkout our contribution page and view our source code on GitHub to get started. Also, visit our HipChat to talk with us!

Game Overview
Objective: Your doomsday device is set to go off in 10 minutes! Don't let the any pesky soldiers or super heroes get to your doomsday device or they will destroy it! (and you lose). To defend your doomsday device you can build towers and monsters to fight off the heroes.
For more details checkout our game guide.
